Capabilities

  • Mindspin: A "roulette-wheel" type attack with a variety of different effects, potentially including mind-control.
  • Psi-reanimate: Brings both dead humans and dead aliens back to life as shambling zombie-esque puppets at full health (sometimes even more than full health). Reanimated targets die once again the moment the Sectoid is slain or control is otherwise interrupted.
  • Psi-Panic: Dominates the will of a target, attempting to force the target to panic.
  • Psi-Disorientation: Dominates the will of a target, attempting to disorient the target.
  • Mind Control: Dominates the will of a target, controlling its actions until the sectoid or the target is killed.
