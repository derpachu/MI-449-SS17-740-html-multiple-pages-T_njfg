Capabilities

  • All Chryssalid melee attacks will cause poison.
  • Organic units killed by poison will become a cocoon. If the cocoon is not destroyed during the gestation period, three new Chryssalids will emerge.
  • Chryssalids have also gained the ability to burrow underground and lie in wait to ambush unsuspecting targets.
